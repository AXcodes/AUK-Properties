AUK Properties is Manchester Based

AUK Properties focuses mainly on Manchester property market. Starting in Salford, expanded into Manchester city centre and surrounding areas. Whether your focus is on high end apartments in Spinningfields or student accommodation in Fallowfield, we are here with all the answers to your needs. And once you are ready for the home or investment hunt in Wilmslow or Altrincham, we can save your time by sourcing properties that best match your budget and requirements. We offer Let Only or viewings services within 10 miles of Manchester City Centre and looking to expand into Liverpool area in the future.
